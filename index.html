Broth, Seswaa, Cake!

Starter

Beef bone broth - Good for the soul broth. When cooking the Seswaa, all the flavours from the meat and bones create a simple and hearty broth. Served with a few herbs, it’s a great start to the meal.

Main

Seswaa and phaletshe - Seswaa is the national dish of Botswana! Served with “phaletshe” or maize porridge, it is a staple at weddings and any big family gathering. A simple but deliciously comforting meal.

Dessert

Sorghum and suji cake - Sweet and sticky. The sorghum and suji (Semolina) mix makes a crumbly textured cake.

Starter

Sorghum herb salad - gluten free and highly nutritional, sorghum makes a healthy and robust salad; mixed with herbs, seasonal vegetables & crispy pumpkin.

Main
